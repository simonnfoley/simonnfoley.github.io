IMT Chaire CyberCNI

Jan, 2016 Simon Foley

The IMT Chaire on the cybersecurity of critical infrastructures, held by IMT Atlantique in Rennes, is a collaboration between three schools of Institute Mines Telecom (IMT Atlantique, Telecom ParisSud and Telecom ParisTech) and eight industrial partners.
